KAPSARC Toolkit for Behavioral Analysis

A Framework for Modeling Collective Decision-Making Processes (CDMPs)

We are releasing a series of papers that describe the assumptions, algebra, and mathematical underpinnings of KTAB. As we develop the code base, we will continue to document the foundations of new models available within KTAB in the KAPSARC Discussion Paper series.

We are also releasing papers that show applications of KTAB models to relevant topics. The papers are available for download on this page, along with a powerpoint summary (often with more detail on the modeling results), and the data use for the analysis.
